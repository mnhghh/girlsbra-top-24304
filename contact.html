How accurate is your calculator?

Our calculator provides a great starting point, but we always recommend trying bras on as sizes can vary between brands.

Do you sell bras?

We don't sell bras directly, but we provide affiliate links to trusted retailers where you can purchase our recommended products.

How often should I measure myself?

Frequently Asked Questions

Bra Fitting Questions

What if my calculated size doesn't feel right?

Bra sizing can vary significantly between brands. Use our calculator as a starting point, then try different sizes and styles to find your perfect fit.

Should I size up or down if I'm between sizes?

Generally, if you're between band sizes, go with the smaller size for better support. For cup sizes, choose based on comfort and coverage preferences.
